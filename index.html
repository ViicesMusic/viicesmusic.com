    About

    Hey world, we're a rock band called Viices from Wellington, New Zealand.

    This was originally a solo project for Jake McComb, but he finally made some friends in late 2020 to make it a full band, with Liam Crawford on drums and Will Jakicevich on bass. Feel free to give us a holler about gigs, music or just to send some abuse.

    Stuff that we like

    • Juliet McLean is a brilliant singer and songwriter.
    • The lovely Sam Johnson at Rhythm Ace Studio
    • Jake also likes dogs, tattoos and his buddy William.
