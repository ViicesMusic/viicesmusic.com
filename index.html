About

Hey world, I'm Jake and I make music as a solo artist under the guise of Viices.

This started this a few years ago and it turns out I quite enjoy it. I've played a few festivals, did a NZ tour and have had a lot of fun. I'm currently based in Wellington, New Zealand, and am fitting this in around study and other musical projects. Feel free to give me a holler about gigs, music or just to send some abuse.
